My First Apartment

After I returned from teaching abroad in Bejing for a year, I didn't have any desire to continue with law school. I fortunately had a great potential job opportunity for a company that I interned with twice in college and decided to move to San Francisco where this company was headquartered to interview and get hired.

It was an incredibly exciting time for me as I moved with my then partner. We found a studio apartment in Palo Alto which felt like a monumental moment since I was officially living somewhere else permanently that wasn't near any of my family or friends at the time. I was nervous at first but excited at the prospect of seeing new things and interacting with different types of people than who I grew up with.

We lived there for almost 3 years and were able to travel all around the different sections of San Francisco and the area surrounding the city. It was great to always have a new sight to check out the skyline or a serene hiking trail as well as experiencing all kinds of food from different cultures. My favorite memory was being able to see a Golden State Warriors game while they were in the playoffs the year they won an NBA title.
