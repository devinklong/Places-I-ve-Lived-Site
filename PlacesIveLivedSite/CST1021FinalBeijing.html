My Life Abroad

After I finished college the first time at the University of Iowa, I had initially planned to go to law school, but wanted to take a break so I filled that year in by teaching kindergarten abroad in Beijing. I wanted to travel somewhere on my own for a longer period of time than before.

It was one of the most challenging, yet accomplished thing I've done with my life It forced me out of my comfort zone in every way imaginable and through that I made lifelong friendships and became a more confident person.

I loved Beijing because it had a perfect mixture of modern and old architecture. There were tons of old temples and palaces you could visit. I was fortunate to be able to teach at an inner city school and enjoyed my daily communte on the subway from the east into the heart of the city.
